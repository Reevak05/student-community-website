The

Student Community Model


The easiest way to set up a student Discord server for your school.

For high school and college students.

Get started

Don't worry about writing rules, verifying emails, or spending countless hours creating channels and roles. Student Community Bot handles almost everything you need for your server:

Automatically creating a channel and corresponding role for every class

Interactive setup procedure for new members to select their classes

Email verification, so only students from your school can access your community

Rules, keeping your community safe and fun for everyone

Information for users

Information for Admins

Administration and Moderation team organizational structure

Completely automated referral role allocation to encourage students to invite others

Nickname management

Language translation between 100+ languages

Computational knowledge engine (WolframAlpha) queries

And more!

All you need is a list of your school's classes and the ability to push a button.

Get started by entering your information here. Due to server constraints, we can't just have a link for you to add the bot, but we'd love to work with you! Fill out the form, and we'll get a Student Community Bot instance set up for you.

Questions? Email us at contact@kaveergera.com.

The Student Community Model gives you access (if you wish) to resources to manage and grow your server. Admins and Mods of other successful deployments are also available for your support. The model can be implemented in existing servers.



Why create a Discord server for your high school or college? It's a fun project, a great way to meet new people, and an invaluable study aid, so anyone can contact members of their class easily when they forget the date the teacher rescheduled the test to.
